Box model: tells us how every element on a webpage is treated as a rectangular box.

4 important concepts(inside to outside):
1. Content: text/image/video | width x height |
2. Padding: space b/w content & border | transparent but it takes white-space
3. Border: Line around the padding and content
4. Margin: space outside the border, b/w the current element & other elements
